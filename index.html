About

I am a creative front-end designer specializing in clean, responsive interfaces.

QUALIFICATIONS

I am a skilled Front-End Designer with over 4 years of experience in creating responsive, visually appealing, and user-friendly web interfaces. My expertise includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap, along with a strong background
in graphic design, allowing me to craft seamless digital experiences that are both functional and aesthetically engaging.

I hold a degree in Computer Science from The Islamia University of Bahawalpur (IUB), as well as additional certifications from TICTAC (TICER) and NAVTTC, further enhancing my technical and creative capabilities. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for design, I specialize in building modern websites that combine cutting-edge front-end technologies with intuitive user interfaces.
